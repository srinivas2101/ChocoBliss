Our Story

Born from a love of fine chocolate and a belief that every bite should be extraordinary.

From Bean to Bliss

ChocoBliss was founded in 2020 with one simple idea: bring the world's finest artisan chocolates to every home in India. We source our cacao from sustainable farms across Ecuador, Ghana, and Vietnam.

Every bar, truffle, and bonbon in our collection is handcrafted by passionate chocolatiers who believe that great chocolate is an art form — one that deserves to be experienced, not just consumed.

Today, with 200+ handcrafted varieties across 8 categories, we're India's most loved online chocolate destination.
